What We Do

Calema Homes

Building tomorrow’s communities today. We develop premium estates and offer land sales across Nigeria, creating secure and modern living spaces for families and businesses.

Igboamaka Rice

Feeding the nation with quality. Igoboamaka farms, distributes, and sells premium-grade rice in bulk, ensuring Nigerians have access to nutritious, locally grown food.

Calema TV

Award-winning storytelling. Since 2018, Calema TV has produced over 75 Nollywood films, earning recognition as Eastern Nigeria’s top movie production company.

Grace and Mercy Hospitals

Excellence in medical care. Located in Umuanuka Otolo Nnewi, Grace & Mercy Hospital provides top-tier healthcare services, ensuring the well-being of our community with state-of-the-art medical facilities.

Calema Football Academy

Nurturing the next generation of football stars. We identify and train talented young athletes, equipping them with the skills and opportunities to succeed in the global football arena.

Chibuzor Njideofor Foundation

About Calema Industries

Calema Industries Ltd is a multi-faceted Nigerian conglomerate committed to driving progress across key industries. With a legacy of excellence, we have established a strong presence in real estate, agriculture, healthcare, entertainment, sports, and philanthropy. From developing thriving communities to producing award-winning films, empowering young athletes, and feeding thousands through our foundation, we are more than a business—we are a force for change.
Our Mission: To create lasting value by delivering quality, innovation, and impact across industries. Our Vision: To be a benchmark of excellence in Africa, transforming lives through sustainable business solutions.
